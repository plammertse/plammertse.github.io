about this site

no rhyme or reason

This site gives musings on things that have caught my attention over the years.

The intention is often to have "simple" basic principles clarify "difficult" subjects.

The selection tends to be purely personal, and largely a matter of taste.

Also, some autobiographical material plays a role in these selections.

contents and organization

This site is really a personal blog. For easier reference, it is organized by subject, instead of chronologically.

Some parts are mere sketches of ideas, others are more elaborate, but all of them are works in progress.

This site contains a serious text on propeller theory, and there will be a similar text on haptics as well.

disclaimer

Please feel free to use any of this material, but do so at your own risk  :
