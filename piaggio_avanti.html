Piaggio Avanti

history

-   P166 Portofino.

-   gull wing pusher because flying boat.

-   turned first into an amphibian, then into a landplane without rethinking.

the Avanti

-   pusher engines, tail and canard.

-   two mistakes in one airplane, both due to unscientific, wishful thinking.

    -   assumed lower trim drag for canard airplanes.

    -   assumed better efficiency of pusher propellers.

noise

I live near an airport where one of these is based. If I hear a nasty siren coming fron a speck in the sky, I know it is the Avanti passing overhead.



P. Lammertse, 2025
