the Questair Venture

a short coupled aircraft

A cautionary tale against short-coupled aircraft, and why they can turn around and bite you if the propeller stops (and therefore no longer acts as a front fin).

verdict

Not a downright dangerous aircraft, but a fine example of an extreme design choice ( small wing and short fuselage ) creating a fast but tricky aircraft, "unstable like an old Bonanza".

Presumably, without properly warning prospective buyers about the risks of extreme design choices.



P. Lammertse, 2025
